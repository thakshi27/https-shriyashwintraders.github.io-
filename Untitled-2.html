Welcome

We provide knowledge and support for vegetable farming in Sri Lanka, including safe and effective use of insecticides, fungicides, and pesticides.

Vegetable Farming in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka has a diverse climate suitable for crops like carrots, beans, cabbage, tomatoes, and brinjal.

  • Use quality seeds
  • Maintain proper irrigation
  • Apply organic compost when possible
  • Monitor pests regularly

Insecticides

Used to control insects that damage crops. Always follow recommended dosages and safety measures.

Fungicides

Help prevent fungal diseases like leaf spots and mildew.

Pesticides

General chemicals used to protect crops. Avoid overuse to protect soil and environment.
